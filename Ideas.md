# History Engine

## Abstract

This is a program that generates a new world based on set conditions, or progresses an existing world through time based on the existing conditions in that world.

As the world progresses and populations grow, there will rarely appear individuals with a higher "specialness" indicating how relevant they are to a potential story.

When a person of sufficiently high specialness appears, conditions for the world at large or a more localized region may change -- such as the invention of a crucial technology, discovery of a kind of magic, development of a revolutionary art style, and so on. If the specialness is beyond a certain threshold, progression of the world halts and user intervention is requested.

When user intervention is requested, the user then uses the state of the world to write a short story taking place in the world as it stands. When done, they make changes to the world state, such as increasing or reducing populations, adding or removing natural disasters and landmarks, giving new tech/magic/etc to a culture, and so on. Then the process continues.
